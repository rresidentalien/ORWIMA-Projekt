About Trainwatchers

Trainwatchers was created as part of my Basics of Web and Mobile Application Development class at FERIT, Osijek. It has an accompanying app for keeping track of trains you've been on or seen. I chose my project to be about trains because I think it's the best form of public transport, and I enjoy seeing different trains when I travel.
