Train travel tips

  • The best way to travel by train from eastern Croatia to other countries in Europe is through Hungary (Hungarian Railways / MÁV). From Osijek there is a direct train to Beli Manastir, from where MÁV runs a direct service to Pécs for only 2.5 euros. Budapest is then only some 7 euros away, and from Budapest you can directly or indirectly get just about anywhere in Europe by train - Austria, Czechia, Slovakia, Romania, Ukraine directly, and then onward wherever you'd like.
